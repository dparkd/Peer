Peer is transforming education by:

Providing Context

Peer has the unique advantage of combining the digital and physical in order to make hard to see or abstract concepts tangible and visible.

Facilitating Prototyping

In reducing the feedback loop, Peer allows students to quickly iterate over their designs so that they can innovate quicker.

Opening Collaboration

By providing a safe and open environment, the students are able to work together and build off of one another's ideas through collaboration.

The technology behind Peer

Internet Enabled Sensors

Depending on the subject lesson, an internet enabled sensor will collect different data types. After collecting data like rotational speed, acceleration, or sound the sensor sends the real data to the cloud to be used in the Mixed Reality experience.

Mixed Reality Experience

After collecting real World data from the sensor, the headset allows the student to view their progress and results in a mixed reality simulation. Using mixed reality opens up the the possibility of meshing the digital and the physical in one experience.
